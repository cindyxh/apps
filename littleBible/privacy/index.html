Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how littleBible ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Information Collection

littleBible does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. We do not require account creation, registration, or login. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Payment or financial information

1.2 Local Data Storage

The app stores data locally on your device only, including:

  • Reading Preferences: Your selected Bible translation, current book and chapter, and font size settings
  • Bible Content: The Bible text database is stored locally on your device

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to use, share, or sell. All app functionality operates entirely on your device using locally stored data.

3. Third-Party Services

littleBible does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates completely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device security features like passcodes or biometric authentication
  • Being cautious about granting permissions to other apps

5. Children's Privacy

littleBible does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any information, this app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All your app preferences and data are stored locally on your device, and you have full control over them. You can:

  • Delete the app at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Clear app data through your device's settings
  • Reinstall the app to reset all preferences
