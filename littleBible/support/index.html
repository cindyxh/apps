Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the Bible translation?
Tap the settings icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the app, then select your preferred translation from the dropdown menu. You can also compare two translations side-by-side by selecting a second translation.
How do I adjust the font size?
Open the settings modal and use the font size slider to adjust the text size to your preference. Your font size preference is saved automatically.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! littleBible works completely offline. All Bible content is stored locally on your device, so you can read anywhere, anytime without an internet connection.
How do I navigate to a specific book and chapter?
Use the book and chapter dropdown selectors at the top of the screen. Simply tap on the current book or chapter name to see all available options.
Does the app save my reading progress?
Yes! The app automatically saves your current book, chapter, selected translation, and font size. When you reopen the app, you'll be right where you left off.
Why don't I see Chinese translations on Android?
If Chinese translations (CUVS/CUVT) don't appear, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. This ensures the database is properly initialized. Make sure you're using the latest version of the app.
Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Yes, you can install littleBible on multiple devices. However, your reading preferences and progress are stored locally on each device and don't sync between devices.
Is my data private?
Absolutely! littleBible does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data (reading preferences, Bible content) is stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Troubleshooting

App Won't Open

  • Make sure you have the latest version installed
  • Try restarting your device
  • Uninstall and reinstall the app

Database Errors

  • Uninstall the app completely
  • Reinstall from the app store
  • This will reset the database and should resolve the issue

Translations Not Showing

  • Ensure you're using the latest app version
  • Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app
  • Check that the translation name appears in the settings dropdown

Font Size Not Saving

  • Make sure you're adjusting the font size in the settings modal
  • Close and reopen the app to verify the setting persists
  • If issues persist, try reinstalling the app
