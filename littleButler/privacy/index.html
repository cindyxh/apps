Privacy Policy

Little Butler - Your Personal Organization Assistant

Last Updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 2025

Introduction

Little Butler ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter in the App remains on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Little Butler does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Financial information (account numbers, balances, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other data from your device

How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter in Little Butler (including term deposits, credit cards, subscriptions, prescriptions, cars, and custom items) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We do not have access to your data
  • No cloud sync or backup services are used
  • No data is transmitted over the internet

Local Notifications

Little Butler uses local notifications to remind you of important dates. These notifications are:

  • Scheduled and stored locally on your device
  • Not transmitted to any external service
  • Only accessible by the App on your device
  • You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings

Third-Party Services

Little Butler does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any other data collection or tracking services

Data Export and Import

Little Butler allows you to export and import your data. This feature:

  • Creates a local file on your device
  • Does not transmit data to any external service
  • Gives you full control over your data
  • Allows you to backup and restore your information

You are responsible for the security of exported data files. We recommend storing them securely.

Children's Privacy

Little Butler is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect any information, this is not applicable, but we want to make our policy clear.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can view, edit, or delete any data at any time
  • You can export your data for backup
  • You can uninstall the App, which will remove all local data (unless you've exported it)
  • You can manage notification permissions in your device settings

Security

While we do not collect or transmit your data, we recommend:

  • Keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric authentication
  • Regularly backing up your device
  • Using the export feature to create backups of your Little Butler data
  • Storing exported data files in a secure location
