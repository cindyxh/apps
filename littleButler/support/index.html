Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new item?

Tap on a category tile on the home screen, then tap the "+" button (or "Add" button) to create a new item. Fill in the required information and tap "Save".

How do reminders work?

Little Butler sends local notifications to remind you of important dates. You can customize when reminders are sent (e.g., 30 days, 7 days, 1 day before). Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings and in the app's Settings page.

Can I customize reminder timing?

Yes! When adding or editing an item, you can configure reminder settings for each date field. You can set multiple reminders (e.g., 30 days, 7 days, and 1 day before) or customize them to your needs.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data. This will create a backup file that you can save to your device or cloud storage. To restore, use the Import Data option.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. It never leaves your device and is not synced to any cloud service. This ensures your privacy and security.

I'm not receiving reminders. What should I do?

  1. Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings
  2. Verify notifications are enabled in the app's Settings page
  3. Make sure you've configured reminders for your items
  4. Check that the reminder dates haven't already passed
  5. Restart the app to refresh reminder schedules

Can I create custom categories?

Yes! Tap "Add Category" on the home screen, then choose "Create New Category". You can define custom fields and set up reminders for any date fields you create.

How do I hide or show categories?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Manage Categories. You can hide categories you don't use, and they'll be available to add back later if needed.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't start

  • Close the app completely and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • If the problem persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app (make sure to export your data first!)

Data appears to be missing

  • Check if you've hidden any categories in Settings
  • Verify you're looking in the correct category
  • If you recently updated the app, try restarting it
  • If you have a backup, try importing it

Reminders not working

  • Check device notification permissions
  • Verify reminders are enabled in app Settings
  • Make sure reminder dates are in the future
  • Check that you've configured reminders for your items
  • Try restarting the app to refresh reminder schedules

Tips & Best Practices

  • Regular Backups: Export your data regularly to avoid data loss
  • Customize Reminders: Set reminders that work for your schedule (e.g., 30 days, 7 days, 1 day before)
  • Use Custom Categories: Create categories for anything you need to track (warranties, licenses, etc.)
  • Keep App Updated: Update to the latest version for bug fixes and new features
  • Review Reminders: Check the Reminders page to see all your scheduled notifications
